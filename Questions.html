How often should I work out?
Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week.
What are the best exercises for weight loss?
Any exercise that gets your heart rate up and makes you sweat can help you lose weight. Include cardio, strength training, and HIIT exercises in your routine.
How much protein should I eat after a workout?
Aim for 0.8 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight per day. After a workout, you can consume 20-30 grams of protein to help your muscles recover.
How can I stay motivated to work out?
Find an activity you enjoy, set realistic goals, and track your progress. Work out with a friend or join a fitness class for extra motivation.
What should I do if I’m injured?
Rest the injured area, ice it, compress it, and elevate it (RICE). Consult a doctor or physical therapist for further guidance.
What are some healthy snacks for before or after a workout?
Choose snacks that are high in protein and carbohydrates, such as a banana with peanut butter, a protein bar, or a Greek yogurt with berries
